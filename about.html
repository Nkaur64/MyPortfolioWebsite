About Me

Hello! I'm Navneet, a beginner web designer passionate about creating clean, user-friendly, and responsive websites. I recently completed the Web Design Core Certificate from NAIT. I earned LinkedIn Learning certifications in Introduction to Web Design and Development and HTML & CSS and JavaScript: Building the Web. My skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Adobe Photoshop.
I’ve built a variety of hands-on projects such as a personal portfolio website, a styled contact form, a to-do list app, Digital Terrarium, and responsive layouts — all focused on usability, accessibility, and clean design. This portfolio reflects my continuous learning journey as I grow my skills and tackle new challenges.

Outside of coding, I enjoy creative design, exploring new tech tools, and helping others. Thank you for visiting my site — stay tuned for more updates as I continue learning and building!
